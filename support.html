Common questions

How do I change the topics I follow?
Open the My Topics tab at the bottom of the app. Tap any topic to follow or unfollow it — changes take effect immediately.

When does my daily digest update?
Your digest is refreshed each morning. You'll receive one notification when it's ready, if you've enabled notifications.

How do I turn off notifications?
Go to your device Settings → Notifications → Tanto and toggle them off. You can also manage this from within the app.

How do I delete my account?
Open the settings sheet by tapping the icon in the top right of the app. Select Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

Where are my bookmarks stored?
Bookmarks are stored securely in your account and stay available even after a story's daily digest expires.
